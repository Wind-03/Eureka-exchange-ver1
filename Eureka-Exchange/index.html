The Narrative Unfolded.

Research Commercialisation

The commercialization of research outcomes faces significant challenges, with success rates generally ranging between 5% and 20%.

Factors such as the need for refinement, limited funding opportunities, and the gap between academia and industry contribute to

this low success rate, but efforts to improve collaboration and support innovation hold promise for increasing successful commercialization.

Market Opportunity

There's a growing need for domain-specific models to unlock the value and achieve the projected global digital twin market size of $48.2 billion by 2026

Research Outcomes

Up to 80% of research outcomes are models which are archived in standalone computing devices and eventually discarded due to our century-old research publication strategy

The Eureka formula.

Our specialised formula guarantees researchers a connection with potential customers using what we call the 4 M's



Model Management

Eureka provides a platform where researchers can securely manage, share, and publish their models.

Model Commercialisation

Researchers and research institutions can require a fee for end-users to access and execute their models.

Model Execution

Eureka provides a cloud-based platform for end-users across industries to access and execute 3rd party models for specific applications.

Model Customisation

End-users can combine multiple models to create a tailored solution for their specific needs.
